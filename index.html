What Carl does

Clarity is
the strategy.

Before you can communicate well, you need to know what you're actually saying. Most marketing problems are positioning problems in disguise.

Carl works with founders and senior leaders to untangle the thinking, find the real story, and build a clear direction — so that everything downstream becomes easier.

  • 01 What the business actually is. Not the elevator pitch. The honest, compelling version — the one that makes people lean in.
  • 02 Why it matters to the right people. Positioning that's felt, not just stated. Sharp, specific, defensible.
  • 03 How to communicate it consistently. A narrative that travels — across channels, across teams, across time.

Carl works at the thinking level. Not execution. Not management. Direction.
